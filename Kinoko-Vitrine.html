Project Overview

Kinoko Travel is a travel-tech startup aiming to simplify trip planning through an intelligent and user-friendly platform. To support the launch, I designed and developed the official showcase website to present the concept, vision and roadmap.

My Role

I fully handled the creation of the Kinoko Travel showcase website, from design to production.

  • UX/UI & Visual Design: I designed the entire interface, visual identity and layout to reflect Kinoko Travel’s brand and values.
  • Front-end Development: I developed the website using ReactJS, focusing on performance, clarity and responsiveness.
  • Back-end Development: I implemented the back-end using NodeJS to support forms, data handling and future scalability.
  • Communication & Launch: I also manage the project’s communication, including the official LinkedIn launch.
    → View LinkedIn launch post

Outcome

The website serves as the public entry point for Kinoko Travel, validating the concept, collecting early interest and laying the foundation for the future application.
