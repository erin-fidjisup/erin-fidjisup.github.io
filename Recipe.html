Project information

  • Category: Professional Recipe Management Application
  • Client: Reboot Conseil
  • Project date: October , 2023
  • Technology: ReactJS, C#, .NET, Azure DevOps

This project involved the development of a professional recipe management application using .NET C# and ReactJS for the front-end. The application was designed to cater to a group of culinary professionals, allowing them to create, share, modify, and organize recipes. Additionally, the application provided features such as menu creation, group management, and more. The main objective of the application was to streamline the process of recipe management for culinary professionals. The key features and functionalities of the application included:

  • Recipe Management: Users could create, edit, and delete recipes within the application. This feature allowed professionals to maintain a centralized database of recipes, making it easy to access and modify them as needed.
  • Recipe Sharing: The application facilitated the sharing of recipes among the professional community. Users could share their own recipes and explore recipes shared by others, fostering collaboration and knowledge exchange.
  • Menu Creation: The application allowed users to create menus by selecting and organizing recipes from the available database. This feature simplified the process of designing menus for various occasions or specific dietary requirements.
  • Group Management: Professionals could create and manage groups within the application. Group members could collaborate, share recipes, and discuss culinary topics, promoting a sense of community among users.
