PIA – Physiotherapy Consultation Website (Shopify)

Project information

  • Category: Brand Identity, UX/UI & Shopify Website
  • Platform: Shopify
  • Scope: Design, Setup, Content, Integrations
  • Website: nzkpzc-en.myshopify.com

Project Overview

PIA is a physiotherapy consultation website built to provide a clear patient journey, support content distribution, and simplify appointment booking. The objective was to deliver a professional, reassuring online presence with a smooth user experience.

My Role

I led the project end-to-end, from brand identity creation to the Shopify website deployment and integrations.

  • Art Direction & Brand Identity: I created the visual identity, including the logo and brand guidelines.
  • UX/UI & Wireframes: I designed the user flows, UI components and page mockups, then ensured faithful integration on Shopify.
  • Shopify Setup & Go-Live: I set up the Shopify store, configured the structure, and published the website online.
  • Content & Documentation: I managed articles, structured documentation, and ensured content clarity and consistency.
  • Integrations: I integrated a booking calendar for appointments, plus protected access features for documents and video resources.
  • Security & Access Control: I implemented document and video protection to control visibility and ensure safe distribution.

Outcome

The result is a live Shopify website that supports a modern physiotherapy practice: strong branding, clean UX, appointment booking, and a structured, protected content experience.
