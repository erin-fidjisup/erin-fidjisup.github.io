Kinoko Travel: Building an AI-Powered Trip Planner from Scratch

Project information

  • Category: Entrepreneurship, Product Design & Full-Stack Development
  • Project Type: Startup Project supported by Pépite ETENA
  • Project date: September 2023 - Present
  • Skills: Business Planning, AI, UX/UI, Market Research, Financial Forecasting

The Challenge

The modern travel planning process is broken. Travelers spend an average of 16 hours organizing a single trip, and 25% give up because of the complexity. Existing tools are fragmented, forcing users to switch between dozens of websites for flights, hotels, and activities.

Our goal with Kinoko Travel was to solve this problem by creating a single, intelligent, all-in-one platform to make trip planning simple and enjoyable.

My Role and Contributions

As a co-founder, my role was comprehensive, covering both business strategy and technical product definition. This project was developed as part of our application to **Pépite ETENA**, a national program that supports student entrepreneurs in France.

My key responsibilities included:

  • Product Vision & UX/UI Design: I defined the core concept of an AI-powered trip planner. I designed the user journey, from initial idea to a fully booked and flexible itinerary, and created the initial product mockups.
  • Market Research & Business Strategy: I conducted in-depth market analysis to identify key user needs (like the demand for customized and eco-friendly travel). I also performed a competitive analysis to position Kinoko uniquely in the market.
  • Business & Financial Planning: I co-authored the entire business plan, including the marketing strategy, go-to-market plan, revenue model (commissions, subscriptions), and created the 3-year financial forecasts.
  • Pitching & Communication: I prepared and delivered project pitches to juries and mentors, including our successful application to the Pépite ETENA program.

Skills Developed and Outcomes

This entrepreneurial project allowed me to develop a wide range of valuable skills beyond traditional development:

  • Business Acumen: I learned to build a complete business case from the ground up, including market sizing, financial modeling, and defining a legal structure (SAS).
  • Strategic Planning: I developed a multi-phase deployment roadmap, starting with a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) and scaling up to a full-featured platform.
  • Product Management: I translated user needs and business goals into clear product features and technical requirements.
  • Team Collaboration & Leadership: Working as a co-founder required strong communication, shared decision-making, and the ability to align on a common vision.
  • Technical Design: I conceptualized the architecture for a complex application involving AI, multiple API integrations, and a real-time user interface.

The result is a solid, well-researched business plan that earned us a spot in the **Pépite ETENA** program, providing us with mentorship, resources, and a network to turn Kinoko Travel into a reality.
